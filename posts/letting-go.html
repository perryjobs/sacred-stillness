Letting Go Through Prayer

June 8, 2025 · Sacred Stillness Blog

Letting go is one of the hardest things we do. But it’s also one of the holiest.

Prayer invites us to hand over what we were never meant to carry alone—fear, control, grief, guilt. The act of surrender is an act of trust.

“Cast your cares on the Lord and He will sustain you.” — Psalm 55:22

In your next quiet moment, pray simply: “God, I release what is not mine to hold. Fill me with peace in its place.”

You were never meant to carry it all. And you don’t have to.
