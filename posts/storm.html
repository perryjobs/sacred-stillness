Stillness in the Storm

June 10, 2025 · Sacred Stillness Blog

Storms in life are unavoidable. But panic is not the only option. Stillness can exist in the center of chaos.

In scripture, Jesus calmed the storm—not always by removing it, but by speaking peace into it.

“Peace, be still.” — Mark 4:39

Whatever you're facing today, whisper these words into your heart: “Peace, be still.”

You don’t have to wait for the storm to pass to rest in God’s calm.
