Sacred Journaling Practices

June 9, 2025 · Sacred Stillness Blog

Journaling isn’t just for writers—it’s a sacred space where you can meet yourself and meet God.

When you write your prayers, fears, gratitudes, and questions, you create room for clarity and healing.

“Write the vision; make it plain on tablets...” — Habakkuk 2:2

Begin with this prompt: “God, here’s what’s on my heart today...” and write freely without editing or judging.

Journaling is prayer on paper. Let it be a gentle habit in your sacred stillness practice.
